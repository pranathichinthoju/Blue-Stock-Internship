# Day 3 Summary – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## Objective
The goal of Day 3 was to perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on mutual fund datasets to understand market trends, investor behavior, and fund performance using visual analytics.
## Work Completed
### 1. NAV Trend Analysis
- Plotted daily NAV movements for multiple mutual funds (2022–2026).
- Identified long-term growth patterns and market volatility phases.
- Highlighted bullish and corrective market periods.
### 2. SIP Inflow Analysis
- Visualized monthly SIP inflows from 2022–2025.
- Observed a consistent upward trend indicating rising retail investor participation.
- Identified strong growth in disciplined investment behavior.
### 3. AUM Growth Analysis
- Compared Assets Under Management across fund houses.
- Found SBI Mutual Fund as the dominant player in the market.
- Visualized fund house-wise dominance using bar charts.
### 4. Category-wise Inflows
- Analyzed inflows across different mutual fund categories.
- Identified higher inflows in Flexi Cap and Small Cap funds during bullish markets.
- Created heatmap for monthly category trends.
### 5. Investor Demographics Analysis
- Studied age distribution, gender split, and SIP investment patterns.
- Majority investors belong to the 26–35 age group.
- SIP investments are higher among working-age population.
### 6. Geographic Distribution
- Analyzed state-wise SIP investments and city tier distribution.
- Maharashtra and Karnataka emerged as top contributing states.
- Tier-1 (T30) cities dominate investment participation.
### 7. NAV Correlation Analysis
- Created correlation heatmap of NAV returns for multiple funds.
- Observed strong correlation among large-cap funds.
- Highlighted diversification patterns in mid and small-cap funds.
## Key Insights
- Mutual fund investments show strong long-term growth trends.
- SIP adoption is increasing steadily across India.
- Large-cap funds are more stable compared to mid/small-cap funds.
- Investment activity is heavily concentrated in urban regions.
- Banking, IT, and energy sectors dominate portfolio allocations.
## Deliverables
- EDA Analysis Notebook with 15+ visualizations
- Clean charts using Matplotlib, Seaborn, and Plotly
- 10 structured business insights
- Fully completed exploratory analysis workflow
## Outcome
Day 3 successfully established a strong analytical foundation for understanding mutual fund performance and investor behavior. The insights generated will support further modeling and dashboard development in upcoming project phases.
